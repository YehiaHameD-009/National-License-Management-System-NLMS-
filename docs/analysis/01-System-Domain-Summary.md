# System Domain Summary

## Project Name

National License Management System (NLMS)

## Project Motto

The Project That Builds The Engineer

## Overview

The National License Management System (NLMS) is a modern licensing platform designed to manage the complete lifecycle of licensing services.

The system initially focuses on driving license services, including license issuance, renewals, re-examinations, replacements, international licenses, and license-related administrative operations.

The project is being developed as a real-world software engineering journey, following professional engineering practices throughout all phases of the software development lifecycle.

---

## Vision

To build a modern national licensing platform capable of supporting multiple licensing domains such as driving licenses and vehicle licenses.

The platform should be scalable, maintainable, easy to use, and suitable for government organizations. It should also be designed to support future integration with modern technologies and intelligent services, including artificial intelligence solutions.

---

## Current Scope

The current implementation focuses on the Driving License Module.

The module supports:

* First-time driving license issuance.
* License renewal.
* Re-examination requests.
* Lost license replacement.
* Damaged license replacement.
* License hold release services.
* International driving license issuance.
* Driver and applicant management.
* License examination management.

---

## Main Actors

### Applicant

A citizen who applies for licensing services through the system.

### Driver

A person who has successfully obtained at least one driving license.

### System User

An employee who operates the system and performs administrative activities such as scheduling examinations and processing applications.

---

## Core Business Areas

### Application Management

Managing service requests submitted by applicants.

### Driver Management

Maintaining driver records and licensing history.

### License Management

Issuing, renewing, replacing, and tracking licenses.

### Examination Management

Managing vision, theory, and practical driving examinations.

### Service Management

Managing available licensing services, fees, requirements, and business rules.
